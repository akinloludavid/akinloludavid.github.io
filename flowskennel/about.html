OUR BRAND STORY - WHY WE DO WHAT WE DO?

I was about ten years old when I started taking care of dogs. I started with a neighbors dog. This four-legged creature thrilled me. Soon my love for this dog extended to other dogs in my neighborhood. I loved them and cared for them as if they were mine while I longed to have mine.

I consider dogs as the most loyal creatures ever formed by God. I love them and I love to care for them. I love to play with them and I love to make them happy. I love to study them and I love to train them.

In my first year at the university, I finally could afford my own dog. It was a great achievement for me. Since then, the journey has been great. I could share the joy of life as much as I wanted with all the dogs in my care.

I believe dogs deserve the best treatment ever. We cannot compensate them enough for their loyalty. I devote more time to them than you can imagine. This is why I started my own kennel.

I urge you therefore to trust my sincere love for dogs and my long years of experience with them. Flow's kennel is indeed one you can trust.

DOGGIE DAYCARE

FLOW'S KENNEL day-care provides a fun-filled and active environment while also fostering good manners and social skills. In our day-care dogs put in our care are engaged in supervised play, benefitting from canine and human socialization and exercise.

FLOW'S KENNEL is designed to fit into our clients’ busy lives. We understand that having a dog in your family is a 24-7 commitment.

The advantage of having a doggie day care is to help dogs socialize with other dogs and humans in order to have a great disposition. The doggie daycare helps our busy clients with active dogs who need extra attention.
